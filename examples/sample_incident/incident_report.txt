TRAFFIC INCIDENT REPORT
=======================

Date: January 15, 2024
Time: 14:30:08 (approximately)
Location: Main Street & Oak Avenue Intersection

SUMMARY
-------
Two-vehicle collision at controlled intersection. Vehicle A (northbound) 
entered intersection on yellow/red light. Vehicle B (eastbound) entered
intersection on green light. Side-impact collision occurred.

VEHICLES INVOLVED
-----------------
Vehicle A:
- Type: Sedan
- Direction: Northbound on Main Street
- Estimated speed at approach: 45 mph
- Estimated speed at impact: 5 mph
- Driver attempted hard braking approximately 4 seconds before impact

Vehicle B:
- Type: SUV  
- Direction: Eastbound on Oak Avenue
- Estimated speed at approach: 25 mph
- Estimated speed at impact: 28 mph (accelerating on green)
- Driver had right of way

SEQUENCE OF EVENTS
------------------
1. Vehicle A approaching intersection at approximately 45 mph
2. Traffic signal for Vehicle A changed to yellow at T-6 seconds
3. Vehicle A driver delayed braking decision by approximately 2 seconds
4. Hard braking initiated at T-4 seconds (speed ~44 mph)
5. Traffic signal changed to red at T-2 seconds
6. Vehicle B entered intersection on green signal at T-0.5 seconds
7. Collision occurred at T=0 (14:30:08.5)
8. Both vehicles came to complete stop within 1 second of impact

DAMAGE ASSESSMENT
-----------------
Vehicle A: Front-right quarter panel damage, airbags did not deploy
Vehicle B: Driver-side door damage, minor airbag deployment

INJURIES
--------
Vehicle A driver: Minor whiplash, transported to hospital for evaluation
Vehicle B driver: Bruising from seatbelt, declined medical transport
Vehicle B passenger: No injuries reported

CONTRIBUTING FACTORS
--------------------
1. Excessive approach speed (45 mph in 35 mph zone)
2. Delayed braking response (2 second delay after yellow light)
3. Short yellow light duration (4 seconds standard)

WITNESS STATEMENTS
------------------
Witness 1 (pedestrian): "The silver car [Vehicle A] was going pretty fast. 
The light had already turned yellow when they were still far from the 
intersection."

Witness 2 (stopped vehicle): "I saw the SUV [Vehicle B] start to go when
their light turned green. Then the other car came through."

OFFICER NOTES
-------------
Weather: Clear, dry conditions
Visibility: Good
Road surface: Dry asphalt
Traffic control: Functioning properly

CITATIONS ISSUED
----------------
Vehicle A driver: Failure to stop at red light, Speeding in school zone
